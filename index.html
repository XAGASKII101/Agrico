Farmer/Driver Registration

Register with us to access new markets and grow your business.

Who Can Register?

  • Farmers
  • Drivers (for logistics and transportation services)

Benefits of Registration

  • Access to new markets
  • Improved market prices
  • Technical support
  • Financial assistance

Registration Steps

Physical Registration:
  1. Visit our office at 123, Agrico Street, Lagos, Nigeria.
  2. Meet with our registration team.
  3. Provide required documents (ID, business license, etc.).
  4. Complete registration form.
Online Registration:
  1. Visit our website at www.agrico.com/register.
  2. Fill out the online registration form.
  3. Upload required documents (ID, business license, etc.).
  4. Submit application.
