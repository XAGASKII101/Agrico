About Agrico Loan Program

Empowering Farmers through Accessible Financing

Agrico Loan Program supports Nigerian and African farmers with affordable loans to improve crop yields, enhance market access, and increase income.

To qualify, farmers must provide all required details, including business registration documents, farming experience, financial records, and valid identification.

Our program offers competitive interest rates, flexible repayment plans, and personalized financial advisory services.
